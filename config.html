Button Configuration

Instructions

- Modify the fields below to your liking.

- Click create button.

- Click on a button to select it.

- You can edit or duplicate the selected button.

- Click in the blue box to deselect your button.

- Double click the blue box to upload an image (helpful for visualizing stream).

- Click submit to send your changes to your stream!

- You can use the button's ID along with Twitch's PubSub to update the buttons without clicking on them.

You should have some knowledge of setting up endpoints on localhost.

- Only 8080 and 3000 are valid ports (ex. http://localhost:8080/mybutton, your port and endpoint would be "8080/mybutton")
